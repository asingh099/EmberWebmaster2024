About Embêr

Hi, I'm Jase. As a second-generation Filipino American, Pinoy flavors have always been a core part of my life. With Embêr, I get to share that savory, yet typically omnivorous experience with a whole new demographic—vegetarians. We’ve worked hard to create plant-based variations of Filipino, Indian, and Chinese dishes through sustainable practices. At Embêr we honor each dishes roots and foster new ones, creating a new standard for taste, tradition, and sustainability all at once.
