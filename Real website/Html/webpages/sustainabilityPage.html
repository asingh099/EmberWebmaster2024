Sustainable Sources

We have a garden in our restaurant and use hydroponic growing technology.
Statistics:

-98% less water usage compared to traditional farms
-Maximizes space usage with vertical growing
-Independent of weather conditions (year round)
-Lower risk of contamination

Local Partnerships

We collaborate with local farmers to bring the highest quality ingredients.

Ember also buys produce including eggs, milk, and flour from local farms. The close distance requires less transportation, thus reducing our carbon footprint. It also allows us to serve the ripest food to our customers since we don't need long distance shipping and freezing. We offer a seasonal menu to give eaters an authentic local flavor. Our kitchen composts our food scraps with worms and sell it to local farmers, completing the cycle.
